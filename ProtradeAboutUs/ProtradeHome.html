AT A GLANCE
Introduction

Headquartered in the Binh Duong Province — 20 km north from the center of Ho Chi Minh City — Protrade Garment JSC is located in the fastest growing and dynamic area in Vietnam. We are situated approximately 40 minutes from the Tan Son Nhat International Airport (SGN) and several seaports.

Protrade Garment JSC holds a leading position in Vietnam garment exports, delivering high quality and fashionable products to our global customers. We specialize in producing dress shirts and jeans with a total capacity of over 10 million units per year. Our commitment to quality and customer service has ensured the successful growth for our many clients.

With more than 28 years of development and experience in the sphere of apparel manufacturing, we have a strong foundation of technical expertise with a workforce of more than 2,500 highly-trained, enthusiastic and creative professionals.

Our company’s work ethic matches Vietnam’s cultural pillars of Unity, Creativity, Cooperation, and Development.

Through excellent teamwork and communication, Protrade Garment JSC is always ready to collaborate with our global customers to create quality products which meet their high expectations.

You, the customer, are the inspiration and driving force for everything we do at Protrade Garment JSC. Our ultimate goal is to provide you with unparalleled quality, collaboration, flexibility, and customer satisfaction!

    • History
    The precursor of Protrade Garment JSC was Protrade Garment Export Enterprises, which was an affiliated unit of Protrade Corporation, founded in 1989.
    On 01/01/2007, based on the Enterprise Law No. 60/2005/QH11, The enterprises became an independent company named Protrade Garment Company Ltd.
